Katherine Forrest Registered Childminder is a Lancaster-based childminder, who looks after children aged between 6 months and 8. Here, there is a caring environment, with plenty of toys, suitable for all ages. Collection from Bowerham School is available, as well as supper and lunch for the children, which is adjustable to suit the nutritional requirements of the children.
